The Problem

The Current Site Shows
Doors. It Doesn't Sell Them.

A photo gallery on a WordPress theme can showcase craftsmanship, but it can't close a sale the way a structured storefront can.

ISSUE 01
No Instant Pricing

Buyers can't gauge whether a door fits their budget without messaging in first. Most simply leave before ever making contact.

ISSUE 02
Unstructured Catalog

A photo grid isn't a shopping experience. There's no way to filter by material, size, or use case, so browsing feels like scrolling, not choosing.

ISSUE 03
Manual Restocking

Every new door design, price change, or sold-out item currently requires a developer to update the site, slowing the business down.

ISSUE 04
Slow, Unoptimized Pages

Heavy, uncompressed product images hurt both search ranking and mobile load times, right where most buyers in this market are browsing.

"Every visitor who leaves without a price never becomes a customer."

The Solution

Four Integrated Subsystems.
One Unified Platform.

Every component is engineered to work in absolute synchronicity, from the public storefront to daily catalog management.

A Subsystem A
High-Fidelity Storefront

A premium, animated public website that communicates craftsmanship and drives client acquisition, with bespoke motion, interactive modals, and full mobile responsiveness.

B Subsystem B
Structured Catalog Engine

A filterable product catalog with individual pages per door, organized by material, size, and use case, so browsing turns into shopping.

C Subsystem C
Instant Quote Engine

A real-time calculator that turns door type, size, material, and finish into a price range, then hands the lead straight to WhatsApp.

D Subsystem D
Admin Command Center

A private dashboard where staff manage products, prices, and stock status, and track every quote request from submission to sale.

Instant Quote Engine

A Price Before
A Phone Call.

Visitors select a door type, material, size, and finish, and get a real, live estimate in seconds. No account. No waiting on a reply. Just enough information to decide whether to reach out.

  • Real-time price range generated from the live catalog, not a guess
  • "Get Exact Quote on WhatsApp" auto-fills the visitor's selections
  • Optional photo upload of the existing doorway for a sharper estimate
  • Every quote request logged and tagged, never lost in a chat inbox
  • Financing/installment messaging shown where it applies
  • Admin can adjust base pricing at any time, no redeploy required
